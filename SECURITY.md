# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

We take the security of DataCore VietNam software and data seriously. If you
believe you have found a security vulnerability, please report it to us
privately. **Do not open a public issue.**

Report vulnerabilities by either:

- Using GitHub's [private vulnerability reporting](https://docs.github.com/en/code-security/security-advisories/guidance-on-reporting-and-writing-information-about-vulnerabilities/privately-reporting-a-security-vulnerability)
  on the affected repository, or
- Emailing **contact@datacore.vn**

Please include:

- A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact
- Steps to reproduce, or a proof of concept
- Affected versions, repositories, or endpoints
- Any suggested remediation

## What to expect

- We will acknowledge your report within **3 business days**.
- We will provide an assessment and expected timeline within **10 business days**.
- We will keep you informed as we work on a fix.
- We will credit you in the advisory once resolved, unless you prefer otherwise.

## Scope

This policy applies to all repositories in the DataCore-VietNam organization.
Please do not access, modify, or exfiltrate data that does not belong to you,
and do not run denial-of-service tests against our systems.
